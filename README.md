# Vanility

AI Powered Harvest Timing and Curing Quality Estimator for Indonesian Vanilla Farmers

## Garuda Hacks 7.0 Submission

Vanility is developed as a software solution for Garuda Hacks 7.0. The event takes place from July 16 to July 18 2026. This platform is designed to support the Indonesian agriculture and food systems sector by empowering local vanilla farmers and cooperatives to capture the true value of their harvests.

## Project Overview

Indonesia is the second largest vanilla producer in the world. However, the nation captures very little of the economic value because vanilla beans are frequently sold raw without proper harvest timing or curing. Vanility addresses this knowledge gap by providing farmers with direct digital access to harvest scheduling and post-harvest curing quality estimation.

## Problem Statement

Indonesia contributes approximately thirty percent of global vanilla production. Despite this massive volume, the country ranks seventh in vanilla export value. Export values have declined significantly due to unstable supply, inconsistent quality standards, and a lack of transparency. 

Around thirty thousand vanilla farmers in Indonesia depend on this crop. Due to financial pressures and theft concerns, many harvest their beans far too early, often at three to four months instead of the ideal eight to nine months. Early harvesting leads to low vanillin content and poor aroma quality, which reduces the market grade and selling price.

Furthermore, traditional curing processes lack temperature and humidity control. The drying and aging steps are often skipped, causing the characteristic vanilla aroma to fade. These inconsistent practices make it difficult for farmers to meet export standards.

## The Solution

Vanility provides a digital platform that helps vanilla farmers determine the perfect harvest window and estimate the quality of their processed vanilla. By using historical data and agricultural guidelines, the application offers:

1. Predictions on maturity status and vanilla grades based on simple production metadata.
2. Step by step guidelines for post harvest curing based on agricultural research and national standards.
3. Financial comparison calculations that illustrate the difference in earnings between selling raw beans and selling cured products.

This approach transforms complex agricultural knowledge into a simple digital tool accessible to farmers and cooperative leaders.

## Core Features

1. Vanilla Maturity and Grade Estimator
Farmers input metadata such as pollination date, location, and details of their curing process. The system provides an estimation of the harvest status, the predicted vanilla grade, and actionable curing steps to improve quality.

2. Value Addition Calculator
This feature displays the potential revenue difference between selling raw vanilla beans and processing them into vanilla extracts or pastes. It helps farmers visualize the economic benefit of post harvest curing.

3. Curing Guidance Checklist
An interactive reference page detailing the correct stages of vanilla curing, including blanching, sweating, sun drying, and conditioning, along with the ideal duration for each stage.

## Target Audience and Social Impact

Vanility focuses on two main groups:

1. Individual Farmers
Farmers who need to know the optimal time to harvest and the correct methods to cure their beans to achieve higher market prices.

2. Cooperative Leaders
Cooperative administrators who collect harvests from multiple members and require a tool to standardize curing guides and forecast batches before negotiating with buyers.

By helping farmers transition from selling low value raw crops to high value cured products, Vanility aims to increase household incomes and support sustainable agricultural growth in Indonesia.
